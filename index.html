Born from a Spark, Built by Three.

How a wild idea took wing.

From Bathroom Wall Scribbles to Sky-High Ambitions

It sounds like a movie plot, right? Jason spots a number on a bathroom wall, texts it with a half-joking "life-changing websites?" idea. What he didn't expect was Mariah, with her infectious "Why stop at websites? Let's aim higher!" energy, and Abhishek, the calm strategist who heard 'airline' and actually started drafting a business plan on a napkin.

One wild text led to a coffee, that coffee to a hundred late-night calls, and suddenly, this unlikely trio – Jason the idea guy, Mariah the visionary spark, and Abhishek the get-it-done architect – were actually building an airline. We're still figuring out who's the official pilot, who makes the best in-flight announcements, and who ties their shoes fastest (it's a long story).

We're still on the hunt for a great mechanic (seriously, call us!), but our mission is clear: make travel more than just a transit. Make it an *experience*. And yes, we finally have enough airplanes. Mostly.
